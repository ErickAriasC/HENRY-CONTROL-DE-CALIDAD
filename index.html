Implementation

The implementation of the ISO 27001 standard involves the commitment of senior management, risk analysis, planning and execution of security controls, internal audits, external certification and a continuous improvement approach, thus guaranteeing the protection of information through risk management and compliance with international standards.

Certification

Identification, evaluation and management of risks is essential. Demonstrating that the organization has a structured approach to identifying and addressing information security risks is crucial for certification.

The organization must demonstrate that it has implemented appropriate controls and procedures to mitigate the identified risks. This involves establishing security policies and procedures, as well as documenting and monitoring their effectiveness.
